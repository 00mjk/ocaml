You can download examples from 

http://gallium.inria.fr/~naxu/research/testcontracts.tar.bz2

This works for me on linux:
[ directories /contracts and /testcontracts are at the same level ]

$tar -jxvf testcontracts.tar.bz2
$cd contracts
$./configure
$make world
$cd ../testcontracts
$make all

To run the generated bytecode files at /testcontracts, e.g. do

$./basic

If it gives error msg saying bad interpreter /usr/local/bin/ocamlrun, 
you can link it to where you installed your ocamlrun by linking, e.g.

$ln /usr/local/bin/ocamlrun /usr/bin/ocamlrun
